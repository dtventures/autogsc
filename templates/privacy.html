Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 9, 2025

Information We Collect

AutoGSC uses Google OAuth to access the following information:

  • Email address - For account identification and authentication
  • Google Search Console data - To identify pages in your sitemap that aren't indexed by Google
  • Indexing API access - To submit URLs directly to Google's Indexing API for faster indexing

How We Use Your Information

We use this information solely to:

  • Connect to your Google Search Console account
  • Scan your sitemaps to find unindexed pages
  • Submit URLs to Google's Indexing API on your behalf
  • Provide you with indexing status reports and analytics

Data Storage

We store minimal data necessary for the service to function:

  • OAuth tokens (encrypted) - Required to access Google APIs
  • Submitted URLs and indexing status - To track your submissions
  • Your email address - For account management

We do not share, sell, or distribute your data to third parties.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • OAuth tokens are encrypted and stored securely
  • All API communications use HTTPS encryption
  • Access to your data is limited to what's necessary for the service

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data at any time
  • Revoke OAuth access through your Google Account settings
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Discontinue use of the service at any time

Third-Party Services

AutoGSC integrates with:

  • Google Search Console API - To access your site's indexing status
  • Google Indexing API - To submit URLs for indexing

Your use of these services is subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
